About Kirsten

Kirsten Yip is a graduate student at Northeastern University’s Bouvé College of Health Sciences, pursuing her Master’s of Science in Human Movement and Rehabilitation Science.

Her research interests involve sports medicine and orthopaedics. More specifically, she is interested in protocols that optimize recovery and injury prevention in the knee joint

She received her Bachelor of Health Sciences (Honours) from McMaster University. During this time, she served as Captain of McMaster's Varsity Cheerleading Team, which is where her research interests originate from.
